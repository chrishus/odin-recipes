Greek Chicken

Greek Chicken has all you need to make a well rounded meal. Tender chicken, tasty vegetables, and delicious orzo pasta, offers a complete meal in just one simple to make dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 1.2 lbs chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

Steps:

  1. To prepare the chicken: In a small bowl, combine the oregano, paprika, salt, black pepper, red pepper flakes, thyme and cinnamon and stire to mix. In a separate bowl, stir together the cornstarch and water.
  2. Select the Saute setting on the Instant Pot and heat the olive oil and garlic for about 3 minutes, until the garlic begins to brown. Add the herb-spice mixture and saute for about 1 minute more. Add the chicken thighs, chicken broth and lemon juice and stir to combine, then arrange the chicken in a single layer.
  3. Secure the lid and set the Pressure Release to Sealing. Press the Cancel button to reset the cooking program, the select the Pressure Cook or Manual setting and set the cooking time for 15 minutes at high pressure.
  4. To make the vinaigrette: While the chicken is cooking, in a jar with a tight-fitting lid or other leakproof container, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, sugar, oregano, salt, black pepper, red pepper and garlic. Cover and shake vigorously to combine, then set aside.
  5. When the cooking program ends, preform a quick pressure release by moving the Pressure Release to Venting. Open the pot and use a slotted spoon the transfer the chicken to a plate. Use two forks to shred the chicken into bite-size pieces.
  6. Press the Cancel button to reset the cooking program and select the Saute setting. Stir the cornstart mixture to recombine, the stir the mixture into the cooking liquid and cook for 2 minutes, until slightly thickened. Press the Cancel button to turn off the pot. Return the chicken to the pot and stire to coat with the sauce.
  7. To prepart the salad: In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, cucumber, bell pepper, tomatoes, onion, olives and feta and toss to mix.
  8. Shake the vinaigrette to recombine, then pout it over the salad. Toss until evenly coated.
  9. Serve the chicken over pasta or steamed rice, with the salad alongside.
