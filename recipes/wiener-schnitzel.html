Wiener Schnitzel

Translation of the name: Wiener this word comes from the word Wien, which is the Austrian city called Vienna. Schnitzel means basically meat in a crust. Serve the schnitzels with salad, ketchup and French fries. It's fucking delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds veal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups bread crumbs
  • â…› cup oil for frying

Directions:

  1. Cut the veal into steaks, about as thick as your finger. Dredge in flour. In a shallow dish, beat the eggs with 1 tablespoon oil, salt and pepper. Coat the veal with egg mixture, then with bread crumbs.
  2. Heat 1/4 cup oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Fry veal until golden brown, about 5 minutes on each side.
  3. Eat that shit.
